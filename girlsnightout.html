GIRLS NIGHT OUT

4 simple yet sweet drink recipes

MOJITO

2 oz white rum
2 oz club soda
6-7 mint leaves
3-4 blackberries
3/4 oz simple syrup
and 1 cup of ice.

In a glass muddle limes, mint leaves, blackberries, and simple syrup. Add rum and ice, and two ounces of club soda. Stir and garnish with mint leaves and blackberry. (Serves 2)

BELLINI

1 oz Peach Shnapps
3 oz dry Champagne
2 oz peach nectar
1/2 cup crushed ice.

Mix the peach nectar and schnapps in a chilled glass. Add half a cup of crushed ice, stir, and add the champagne. May be prepared in a blender to serve frozen drink. (Serves 2)

SANGRIA

1 bottle dry white wine
1/2 cup raspberry vodka
1/4 cup brandy
1 cup mixed berries
1 cup lemon lime soda.

In a pitcher, combine wine, soda, vodka and brandy. Put berries into the bottom of each glass and muddle. Fill glass with ice and stir.

COSMOPOLITAN

1 1/2 fluid ounces lemon vodka
1 fluid ounce Cointreau
1 fluid ounce cranberry juice
2 teaspoons lime juice
Long thin piece orange zest.

Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Add vodka, Cointreau, cranberry and lime juice. Shake to combine and chill. Strain the cosmopolitan into a chilled glass. Add the orange zest over the drink and serve.
