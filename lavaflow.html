LAVAFLOW RECIPE
  • 1 ounces light rum
  • 2 ounces pineapple juice
  • 6 ounces pina colada mix
  • 1 ounce strawberry puree
  • 1 small chunk pineapple to garnish

Blend rum, pineapple juice, and pina colada mix with 1 cup of ice. Pour strawberry puree in a glass. Then Pour in colada blend. Garnish with a pineapple chunk, and serve.

Please refer to our Safe Serve page for appropriate alcohol intake
